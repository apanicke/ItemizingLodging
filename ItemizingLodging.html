Itemizing Lodging in Chrome River

Lodging Receipt:

Steps:

  1. Create the Expense Report by clicking on the "+New" button

  2. Click on the Lodging Mosaic

  3. Complete the fields as in the example below and then click "Save"

  4. Click ‘Itemize’ to break out the charges. Notice that the Total Amount is $670 and the Remaining amount is $670. Therefore each expense will need to be itemized to total the $670.

  5. Click ‘Recurring’ to add the daily room rate and daily taxes.

  6. Enter the required information and click ‘Save’. NOTE: The Room Rate and the Hotel, Taxes, Fees are DAILY rates.

  7. Notice now that the Remaining amount is now $10. Click ‘Itemize’ again.

  8. Click ‘Parking’ to enter the final $10.

  9. Enter the information, click ‘Add Attachments’ to add the hotel receipt. Click ‘Save’. NOTE: in the case of parking, it is the total fee.

  10. The lodging fee has now been fully itemized. Add other expenses or if there are no other expenses to add, you are ready to submit.
